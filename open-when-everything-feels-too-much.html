you’re overwhelmed and that’s valid. life doesn’t come with a pause button, but if you need to take a breath — take it. right now. inhale. hold. exhale. again. slowly. you don’t have to pretend it’s all okay. you don’t have to “handle it all” like some superhero. you’re human — beautiful, fragile, resilient. try this: close your eyes. unclench your jaw. feel your shoulders drop a little. remind yourself: i am doing what i can. the world might feel like it’s crashing down, but you don’t have to carry all of it alone. just one small thing at a time. that’s how we survive the storms. you don’t have to solve everything right now. sometimes surviving the day is already enough. isa-isa lang, mahal.
