minsan, pagod ka na pero wala kang choice kundi magpatuloy. hindi mo kailangan maging okay palagi. pwede ka munang huminga. please remember: the fact na nandito ka pa means you’re stronger than you think. Yung tipo ng pagod na hindi lang physical — yung soul mo na rin yung pagod. Paulit-ulit mo nang sinasabi sa sarili mong “kaya pa ‘to,” pero minsan hindi mo na alam kung naniniwala ka pa talaga. Pero please, wag mo sanang kalimutan na just choosing to stay alive today — that’s already something. Hindi mo kailangang magpaka-superhuman. Pwede kang mapagod. Pwede kang umiyak. Pwede kang huminto sandali. Di ka mahina. Di ka failure. Tao ka. At mahal ka pa rin kahit hindi ka okay. If today all you did was breathe — I’m proud of you for that. you don’t have to carry everything alone. not this time.
