wala kang nararamdaman pero ang sakit pa rin. weird, ‘di ba? Yung tipong wala kang nararamdaman, tapos naiiyak ka kasi wala kang nararamdaman. Gets kita. Minsan akala natin mas okay nang wala kang maramdaman kesa naman masaktan ka ulit. Pero ang hirap din pala. Yung parang may kulang. Parang wala ka na sa sarili mo. Pero, hindi ka sirang tao. Normal yan. Defense mechanism ng utak mo. And you don’t have to “fix” yourself agad-agad. Kahit wala kang maramdaman ngayon, hindi ibig sabihin nun wala kang worth. You're still lovable. You're still human. The feelings will come back, promise. One day, mararamdaman mo ulit yung peace. Yung joy. Yung love. Hinihintay ka lang nun. that emptiness you feel… it doesn’t mean you’re broken. minsan ‘yun yung defense ng utak mo kasi pagod ka na. you’re still here. and that matters.
