bakit parang lahat may progress na... tapos ikaw, stuck?

nakikita mo yung success ng iba, and kahit happy ka for them, hindi mo maiwasang ma-pressure. ang sakit sa loob, ‘no? parang ikaw na lang ang hindi umaabot kahit sobrang effort mo na.

but love, success is not a race. and your timeline is not broken.

yung ibang tao mabilis ang takbo — good for them. pero ikaw, you’re moving with meaning. and slow progress is still progress.

di mo kailangan sumabay sa oras ng iba. your life is not late. you are right on time.

trust that your path will bloom — slowly, silently, beautifully — just like you.
