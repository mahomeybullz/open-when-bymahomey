parang sumisigaw ka pero walang nakakarinig, no? Ang sakit no? Yung andaming tao pero parang wala namang totoong nakikinig. Nagsasalita ka, pero parang palaging may filter. ‘Di mo masabi lahat. Takot kang ma-judge. Takot kang matawag na dramatic. Pero eto — makinig ka. Yung nararamdaman mo, totoo. Yung sakit mo, valid. Kahit walang ibang umintindi. Hindi mo kailangang i-dumb down yung feelings mo para lang matawag na "okay." Deserve mong marinig. Deserve mong maramdaman na may nakikinig sayo. And for now, ako ‘to. Nakikinig. Walang judgment. Kahit dito lang — you’re safe. i hear you. kahit hindi mo masabi ng buo. kahit magulo. you're allowed to feel unheard. but just know, somewhere, someone would’ve stayed if they knew.
