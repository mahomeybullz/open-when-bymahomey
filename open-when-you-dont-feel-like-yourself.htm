parang hindi ikaw ‘yung gumising kanina, no?

you looked in the mirror and something felt… off.
wala kang gana. wala kang energy. parang nagising ka sa ibang katawan.
and for a second, you thought — “asan na ‘yung ako?”

love, there will be days like this.
days where you don’t feel like the version of yourself you used to be.
and that’s okay. normal ‘yan.
hindi ka weird. hindi ka nawawala. nagbabago ka lang.

change is uncomfortable.
especially when it’s happening within you.
you’re shedding versions of yourself that no longer fit.
and of course it’s going to feel unfamiliar.
you’re grieving who you were, and that’s a kind of heartbreak too.

pero hindi ibig sabihin nun na hindi mo na kilala sarili mo.
hindi ibig sabihin nun na wala nang halaga ‘yung mga dating parts mo.
lahat ng versions mo — the joyful, the angry, the unsure, the tired, the healing —
they all existed for a reason.

this version? the one that feels lost?
she’s just in between chapters.

kaya hayaan mong maging gulo muna.
hayaan mong hindi mo muna masagot yung tanong na,
“sino ba talaga ako ngayon?”

trust that even in this in-between —
you are still you.

soft. trying. growing.
always worth loving.

and eventually, babalik din ‘yung feeling na “ako ulit ‘to.”
