iyak ka lang. kahit walang rason. kahit walang words. kahit paulit-ulit na. minsan kailangan mo lang talaga ilabas.

you don’t have to be okay right now. hindi mo kailangang i-explain ‘yung lungkot. basta andito ako, tahimik lang, pero buo ‘yung yakap.

you’re not dramatic. you’re not weak. your tears are valid. your pain is real.

after mo umiyak, please drink water. please huminga. please remind yourself that this feeling — no matter how heavy — will pass.

and while you wait for it to pass, let yourself rest. I’m proud of you for letting yourself feel. that takes so much strength already.
