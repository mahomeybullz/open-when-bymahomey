minsan kahit okay ka na, kahit feeling mo healed ka na… may araw pa rin na natatamaan ka.

isang story, isang comment, isang memory — tapos parang nagcrash lahat ng confidence mo.

suddenly you're too much, or too little, or too awkward, or too loud. ang bilis talaga mag-shift ng utak kapag insecure ka, ‘no?

pero love, your worth doesn’t shrink just because you’re doubting it right now. insecurity lies. it makes you forget the truth.

and the truth is: you are enough. you are kind. you are soft. and you are not replaceable.

hindi mo kailangang maging perfect para maging lovable. hindi mo kailangang magbago para lang ma-validate.

okay lang malunod minsan sa doubt. basta ‘wag mo hayaang manatili ka doon.

kasi sa dulo ng araw, ikaw pa rin ‘yan — strong, sensitive, still trying.
