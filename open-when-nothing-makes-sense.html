minsan parang lahat kalaban mo. time, sarili mo, utak mo, mundo. gusto mong maintindihan lahat pero parang wala kang mahawakan na solid.

and that’s scary, I know. pero love, not understanding everything doesn't mean you're failing. it means you're in the middle of change.

growth looks confusing up close. pero every messy emotion, every "hindi ko alam," every "ayoko na" — part ‘yan ng paglago mo.

hindi mo kailangang ayusin lahat ngayon. hindi mo kailangang i-force yung clarity. sometimes, all you can do is breathe and try again tomorrow.

and that’s already more than enough.
